# Refund and Cancellation Policy

## Overview

This document outlines our refund and cancellation policy for the Homeschool LMS subscription service.

## Subscription Plans

- **Free Plan**: $0/month - No payment required, no refunds applicable
- **Basic Plan**: $10/month or $108/year - Includes 14-day free trial

## Free Trial Period

### 14-Day Free Trial

- All new Basic plan subscriptions include a **14-day free trial**
- No payment is required to start the trial
- You can add a payment method at any time during the trial
- If no payment method is added, the subscription will be automatically canceled when the trial ends
- You retain full access to Basic plan features during the trial period

### Trial Cancellation

- You may cancel during the trial period at any time
- No charges will be applied if you cancel before the trial ends
- All your data will be preserved; you'll revert to the Free plan

## Regular Subscription Cancellation

### How to Cancel

1. Navigate to the **Subscription** page in your account
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"** to open the Stripe Customer Portal
3. Select **"Cancel Subscription"**
4. Confirm your cancellation

### What Happens When You Cancel

- Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
- You will continue to have access to all Basic plan features until that date
- After the billing period ends, your account will automatically revert to the Free plan
- **No refunds are provided for partial months or unused time**
- All your data (students, evidence, reports) will be preserved

### Example

- You subscribe on January 1st (monthly plan)
- You cancel on January 15th
- You keep access until February 1st (end of paid period)
- Starting February 1st, you're on the Free plan
- No refund for January 15-31

## Refund Policy

### General Policy

**We do not offer refunds for:**
- Partial month subscriptions
- Unused time in a billing period
- Change of mind after the trial period
- Failure to cancel before renewal

### Exceptions

Refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

1. **Billing Errors**: If you were charged incorrectly due to a system error
2. **Service Outages**: Extended service unavailability (7+ consecutive days)
3. **Unauthorized Charges**: Charges made without your authorization

### How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund under one of the exceptions:

1. Contact us at [support email] within 7 days of the charge
2. Provide:
   - Your account email
   - Transaction ID (from your email receipt)
   - Reason for refund request
   - Supporting evidence if applicable

3. We will review and respond within 5 business days
4. Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days

## Payment Failures

### Failed Payments

- If a payment fails, your subscription status will change to **"Past Due"**
- You will receive an in-app notification and email (if configured)
- You have a grace period to update your payment method
- After multiple failed attempts, Stripe may cancel your subscription

### Updating Payment Methods

1. Go to **Subscription** page
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"**
3. Update your payment method in the Stripe Customer Portal
4. Your subscription will be reactivated once payment succeeds

## Plan Changes

### Upgrading (Free → Basic)

- Takes effect immediately
- You gain access to all Basic features right away
- 14-day free trial applies to first-time subscribers

### Downgrading (Canceling Basic → Free)

- Takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- No refunds for remaining time
- Data is preserved, but limits apply

### Switching Billing Frequency (Monthly ↔ Annual)

- You can switch between monthly and annual billing via the Customer Portal
- Prorated charges/credits apply based on unused time
- Managed automatically by Stripe

## Data Retention

### After Cancellation

- **All your data is retained** when you downgrade to Free
- You can still view:
  - All student profiles (but can only actively use 1)
  - All evidence uploaded (but can't add more beyond 15)
  - All previously generated reports
- You cannot:
  - Add more than 1 student
  - Upload more than 15 evidence items total
  - Generate new reports

### Account Deletion

If you wish to permanently delete your account and all data:

1. Cancel your subscription first
2. Contact us at [support email]
3. Request account deletion
4. We will delete all your data within 30 days
5. **This action is irreversible**
